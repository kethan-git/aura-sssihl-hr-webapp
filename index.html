About AURA

Employee attrition is one of the most expensive and preventable challenges facing organizations today. AURA was built to change that.

AURA — Attrition Understanding & Retention Analytics — is an end-to-end HR intelligence platform that transforms raw workforce data into clear, actionable insight. Rather than reacting to resignations after the fact, AURA puts you ahead of the curve: surfacing at-risk employees early, diagnosing root causes across departments, and giving HR leaders the evidence they need to intervene with confidence.

At its core, AURA combines a logistic regression model trained on 5,000 employee records with interactive scenario simulation and AI-generated analysis. The result is a platform that speaks the language of both data scientists and HR practitioners — no technical expertise required to extract meaningful value.

🎚️ Scoring Guide
How to normalise the 4 score columns
Job_Satisfaction 0.0 → 1.0
Divide your survey scale raw score by the max (e.g. 4/5 = 0.80). For Likert: Strongly Disagree = 0.0, Strongly Agree = 1.0.

Use MBI or Oldenburg BBI. Average sub-scores and normalise. For 1–6 MBI scale: (raw − 1) / 5. Higher = more burnout = higher attrition risk.
